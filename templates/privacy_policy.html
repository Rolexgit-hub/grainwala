Like many other websites, log files are stored on the web server saving details such as your IP address, browser type, referrer page, and time of visit. This information is never used to identify individual users and is only for analytics and security.

Cookies & Personal Data

Cookies are small files stored by your browser that allow your preferences to be remembered by the website. They help track site usage and improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser anytime—but some site features may not work properly.

Your Rights
