FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order on GrainWala?
You can track your order by clicking on the “Track Order” menu item and entering your Order ID associated with your purchase.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept secure online payments via UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and Cash on Delivery for eligible products and locations.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Yes, you can cancel or modify your order before it is shipped by contacting our customer support team as soon as possible.
How are refunds processed on GrainWala?
Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days after we receive and confirm the return of your product to your original payment method.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach us by emailing support@grainwala.com or calling +91-8050797979.
