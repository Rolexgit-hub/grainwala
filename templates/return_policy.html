Return & Refund Policy

Shop with confidence at GrainWala! Our policies are transparent, hassle-free,
and designed to give you the best experience possible.

Returns

If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, you can request a return within 7 days of receiving the item.
Products must be unused, in original packaging, and include all accessories, labels, and bills for a successful return.

Refunds

After we receive and inspect your returned item, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.
Refunds will be credited to your original payment method. In case of partial returns, only the value of the returned item will be refunded, excluding any shipping charges, if applicable.

Exchanges

To exchange an item, contact our support team within 7 days of delivery. Items for exchange must meet the return conditions described above.

Return Process

  1. Email us at support@grainwala.com with your order ID and reason for return.
  2. Our team will review your request and share the return shipping details.
  3. Carefully pack the product and send it to the provided address.
  4. After inspection, your refund/exchange will be initiated as per policy.

Exceptions & Non-Returnable Items

Certain goods are exempt from being returned, including perishable products (fresh fruits, vegetables), items marked non-returnable, or products used/damaged after delivery.
